# BG Fashion Performance Dashboard

## Overview
A Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance across 11 territories 
for BG Fashion, covering 104 stores and $112.56M in total annual sales.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel

## Key Metrics
- Total Annual Sales: $112.56M
- Store Count: 104
- Avg Sales Efficiency: $51.03
- Correlation Coefficient (Selling Area vs Sales): 79%

## Key Insights
- OH and PA are the top performing territories
- Strong positive correlation (79%) between store size and sales
- Fashions Direct drives 64% of annual sales
- Competitive territories still contribute 23% of total sales

## Recommendations
- Expand investment in OH and PA
- Prioritize larger retail spaces in future expansion
- Investigate underperforming territories like DE and TN
- Reduce overreliance on Fashions Direct
